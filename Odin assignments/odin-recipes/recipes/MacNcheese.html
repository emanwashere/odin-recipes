Mac'n Cheese Recipe

Description


A classic comfort food made with tender macaroni pasta coated in a creamy, cheesy sauce, often baked with a golden breadcrumb topping.

ingredients

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • - 2 cups milk
  • - 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • - ½ cup breadcrumbs (optional, for topping)
  • - Salt & pepper to taste

Prep

  1. Cook macaroni until al dente, drain.
  2. Make a roux: melt butter, whisk in flour, cook briefly
  3. - Slowly add milk, whisking until thickened.
  4. - Stir in cheeses until melted into a smooth sauce.
  5. - Combine pasta with sauce.
  6. - (Optional) Transfer to baking dish, sprinkle breadcrumbs, bake at 180°C (350°F) for 20 minutes until golden.
