Chicken Alredo Recipe

Description


A rich Italian pasta dish featuring fettuccine tossed in creamy Alfredo sauce, topped with tender pan-seared chicken.

ingredients

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1 lb boneless chicken breast
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1½ cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ¼ tsp nutmeg
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • - 1½ cups heavy cream

Prep

  1. - Cook fettuccine until al dente, reserve some pasta water.
  2. Season chicken with salt, pepper, paprika; pan-sear in olive oil until cooked, slice
  3. - In the same pan, melt butter, sauté garlic.
  4. - Add cream, simmer until slightly thickened.
  5. - Stir in Parmesan and nutmeg; adjust consistency with pasta water if needed.
  6. - Toss pasta in sauce, top with sliced chicken and parsley.
