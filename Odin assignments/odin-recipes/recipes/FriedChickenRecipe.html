Fried Chicken Recipe

Description


Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken pieces (legs, thighs, wings)
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • - 1 cup all-purpose flou
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
    • - 1 tsp salt
  • - Vegetable oil for frying

Prep

  1. - Marinate chicken in buttermilk for at least 1 hour.
  2. - Mix flour, paprika, salt, and pepper in a bag or bowl
  3. Coat chicken pieces in flour mixture
  4. - Heat oil in a deep pan.
  5. - Fry chicken until golden brown and cooked through (internal temp ~75°C / 165°F).
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve hot.
